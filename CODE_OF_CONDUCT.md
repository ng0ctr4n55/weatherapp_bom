# Code of Conduct

This project is committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## 🤝 Our Principles

We expect all participants to treat each other with respect and professionalism. Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, or any form of abusive behavior will not be tolerated.

**What is NOT allowed:**
*   Harassment (including sexual harassment).
*   Discriminatory comments or behavior.
*   Trolling or disruptive behavior.
*   Personal attacks.

**What IS encouraged:**
*   Constructive criticism and thoughtful discussion.
*   Asking "stupid questions"—there are none!
*   Celebrating successes and helping newcomers.

## 🛡️ Enforcement

If you see behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it immediately to a core maintainer or via [Link to private reporting channel, e.g., email@example.com].

Maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove participants who violate this code, which may include banning accounts or revoking write access.

## ⚖️ Accountability

By participating in this project, you agree to abide by this Code of Conduct.

*We believe that a positive and respectful community is essential for building great software.*
